# Elixir Commandor
This is a user specific architecture to control and manage water supply in an organization. The proposed system consist of three nodes i.e, one master and two slave nodes adhering to the architecture of the organization. Master nodes monitors the water levels in Tank - 1 and Tank - 2, transmits data to slave notes through Wi-fi. Slave - 1 positioned close to well detects for the flow of water through the pipeline using flow sensor. Slave - 2 positioned close to sump monitors the water levels in the sump. Slave nodes are powered to control their respective motors say Well motor and Sump motor enabling also enabling to communicate among them. The master node is equipped with Long Range module HC-12 to send immediate updates about the water levels and their working states to the intended user. Keeping the cost and my experience in mind, I was provided with low cost ESP32 microncontrollers to establish network and a pair of solenoid vales to control the flow of water among the tanks.
